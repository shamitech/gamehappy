Introduction

GameHappy ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile games and related services.

Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information You Provide: Game progress, usernames, and preferences you enter while playing our games
  • Automatically Collected Information: Device identifiers, operating system, game version, and gameplay data
  • Contact Form Data: Any information you submit through our contact form for support purposes

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and improve our games
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • To track gameplay analytics and user behavior to enhance game experience
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To prevent fraud and ensure fair gameplay

Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We recommend that you use a strong password and keep it confidential.

Advertising

Our games may display advertisements served by third-party advertising networks. These ad networks may collect data about your device and your interaction with advertisements. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of our advertising partners.

Children's Privacy

GameHappy games are designed for players of all ages. We comply with applicable laws regarding children's online privacy. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13 without parental consent, please contact us immediately.

Third-Party Links

Our games and website may contain links to third-party services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our contact form or email us with your concerns.
