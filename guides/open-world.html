Getting Started in the Open World

The open world is vast and can be intimidating at first. This guide will help you navigate efficiently, complete quests effectively, and maximize your rewards.

Your First Steps

  • Complete the tutorial quests to learn basic mechanics
  • Explore the starter area fully before moving to higher-level zones
  • Gather resources early‚Äîthey're valuable for crafting and selling
  • Join a guild or community group as early as possible for assistance and tips
  • Mark important locations (shops, quest givers, fast travel points) mentally

Quest Completion Strategy

Types of Quests

  • Main Quests: Drive the story forward, unlock new areas, usually required for progression
  • Side Quests: Optional, provide gold and experience, sometimes unlock unique rewards
  • Daily Quests: Reset daily, provide steady income, good for grinding
  • Guild Quests: Require guild membership, often have group objectives

Prioritization Framework

Quest Priority System:
  1. Main quests blocking your progression
  2. Main quests that unlock new areas or NPCs
  3. Side quests with unique rewards (special items, abilities)
  4. Daily quests for consistent gold income
  5. Optional side quests when you have free time

Efficient Quest Tracking

  • Accept multiple quests at once - many are in the same area
  • Complete them "cluster by cluster" to minimize travel
  • If a quest requires travel to a distant location, check if other quests are there too
  • Use fast travel points liberally - you've already unlocked them

Gold Farming Strategies

High-Income Gold Sources (Per Hour)

  • Completing Main Quests: Best gold/time ratio early game
  • Daily Quests: Consistent, reliable income once unlocked
  • Resource Gathering & Selling: Steady passive income, requires minimal skill
  • Crafting & Flipping: Buy raw materials, craft items, sell for profit
  • Dungeon Runs: Higher risk but higher reward for mid-level players
  • Merchant Trading: Buy low from one area, sell high in another (advanced)
üí° Pro Tip: The best gold farming activity depends on your level. Early game: do main quests. Mid-game: run daily quests and gather. Late game: dungeon runs and trading.

Passive Gold Generation

While active farming generates more gold, these methods require less active play:

  • Workshops: Set up crafting to run while you're offline
  • Rental Properties: Unlock housing for steady rent income
  • Shop Stands: Place items for sale in marketplace for percentage-based gold generation

Achievement Hunting

Achievement Categories

  • Exploration Achievements: Visit specific locations, full-map discovery
  • Combat Achievements: Defeat certain enemies, boss victories, combo achievements
  • Collection Achievements: Gather items, complete sets, cookbook completion
  • Social Achievements: Guild rank, friend count, trading volume
  • Time-Based Achievements: Daily streaks, playtime milestones

Efficient Achievement Strategy

  1. List all achievements and their requirements
  2. Identify achievements with multiple-quest requirements (bundle them)
  3. Identify "freebie" achievements (just for playing normally)
  4. Schedule difficult achievements for when you have dedicated time
  5. Join achievement hunting groups for group-based achievements

Early Achievable Milestones

  • Exploration achievements from starter zone
  • First dungeon clear achievement
  • Resource gathering milestones
  • Early combat achievements against starter enemies

Area Progression Guide

Recommended Level Order

Open worlds typically have zones designed for certain character levels. Trying to progress too fast will frustrate you with impossible enemies. Progress zone-by-zone:

  • Starter Zone (Level 1-10): Tutorials, basic quests, learning
  • Early Zone (Level 10-20): First challenging enemies, basic dungeons
  • Mid Zone (Level 20-35): Diverse quest types, group dungeons unlock
  • High Zone (Level 35+): Challenging enemies, raid dungeons, endgame content

Leveling Efficiently

  • Do ALL main quests in current zone before moving to next
  • Do high-experience side quests while leveling
  • Skip content that doesn't yield good experience for your level
  • Group with other players in dungeons for shared experience boost

Resource Management

What to Gather & What to Skip

  • Always gather: Rare materials, quest items, crafting components
  • Gather when inventory has space: Common materials (might be needed later)
  • Skip: Trash items that sell for minimal gold vs. carrying capacity

Inventory Management

  • Regularly sell or discard low-value items
  • Store resources in bank for later use
  • Keep quest items in separate inventory section
  • Upgrade inventory space when available and affordable
